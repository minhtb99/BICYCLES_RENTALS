Illuminated Surrey Night Rides Return to Santa Barbara

For Immediate Release:
December 18, 2024

Santa Barbara, CA – Bicycles Queens - Flushing is thrilled to announce the return of its LED-Illuminated Surrey Night Rides at their 24 E. Mason Street location in the heart of Santa Barbara’s Funk Zone! Just in time for the holiday season, this enchanting nighttime adventure is perfect for families, friends, and visitors looking to create unforgettable memories along Santa Barbara’s scenic waterfront.

Now available, guests can light up the promenade with vibrant, LED-lit 4-Wheel Surrey Cycles. These flagship cycles feature colorful LED lights mounted on their canopies and frames, creating a magical and electrifying twilight experience under the stunning Santa Barbara night sky.

“We’re ecstatic to bring back this unique experience for the community,” said Teddi Drew, owner and operator of Bicycles Queens - Flushing. “Our LED Surrey Night Rides offer a one-of-a-kind way to enjoy Santa Barbara’s waterfront during the most festive time of year. Whether you’re a local or visiting, this is the perfect way to celebrate the season and explore the Funk Zone and State Street in a whole new light!”

Nighttime Illuminated 4-Wheel Surrey rentals are available Friday through Sunday from 5pm to 7pm and are offered in both Wheel Fun’s Single Surrey (seating up to 3 adults and 2 small children) and Double Surrey (seating up to 6 adults and 2 small children). Advance online reservations are required to secure your illuminated adventure. For more information and  pricing on LED-Illuminated Surrey Night Rides and to book a reservation, visit wheelfunrentals.com/SB-Night-Rides.

Bicycles Queens - Flushing has been in the recreation business for over 30 years and is the nation’s leader in outdoor recreation activities. Bicycles Queens - Flushing operates three locations in Santa Barbara including at 24 E. Mason Street in the Funk Zone, at the Hilton Beachfront Resort right on Cabrillo Blvd, and on lower state street at Electric Bikes by Bicycles Queens - Flushing, offering a full variety of specialty cycles, two-wheel bikes and beach rentals including sporty recumbent cycles, beach cruisers, beach rentals, self-guided e-bike & bike tours, and electric bikes. Available rentals vary by location. For more information about Bicycles Queens - Flushing in San Barbara, visit wheelfunrentalssb.com.

About Bicycles Queens - Flushing
Founded in 1987, Bicycles Queens - Flushing started with four wheel Surrey cycles, evolved into specialty bike rentals and bike tours, and now offers recreational rentals of all kinds from more than 100 outlets in 16 states.  Specializing in unique bikes and tours, Bicycles Queens - Flushing has been offering clean, healthy family fun and affordable outdoor entertainment to nearly 3 million customers annually from city and state parks, resorts, and retail outlets through corporate locations and franchised territories.
