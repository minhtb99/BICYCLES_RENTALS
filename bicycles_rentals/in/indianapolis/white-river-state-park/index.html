Welcome to

Bicycles Queens - Flushing

White River State Park

Welcome to Bicycles Queens - Flushing at White River State Park. Intrepid adventurers can rent a range of vehicles, including Surreys, city bicycles, and electric bikes. Or feel like a kid again as you achieve low-to-the-ground glory with a Chopper, Quad Sport, or Deuce Coupe.

Tour downtown Indianapolis with our Self-Guided Bike Tours – offered for our two-wheeled bikes and electric bikes! Bicycles Queens - Flushing also offers rentals for group activities and discounts! Come and enjoy countless shops and restaurants that line the Central Canal and experience everything Downtown Indianapolis has to offer.
