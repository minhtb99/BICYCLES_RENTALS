FDR Boardwalk at Sand Lane

We offer top-of-the-line bicycles and beach rentals on Staten Island. Professional staff will help you select from our variety of specialty cycles, cruiser bikes, tandem bikes, and more. We also offer a self-guided bike tour. We’ve maintained the same pricing since 2018 and continue to be a great value.

You’ll find plenty of opportunities to relax, sightsee, have a snack, and most importantly, have FUN on Staten Island with bike rentals and beach rentals by Bicycles Queens - Flushing at Sand Lane! Whether you’re a local or visiting Staten Island for the first time, don’t miss this unique way to explore this hidden gem on the Atlantic Ocean!

Staying physically active is one of the best ways to keep your mind and body healthy. Low-contact outdoor activities like bike riding is a great way to get your body moving, and relieve stress while getting fresh air.
