Welcome to

Bicycles Queens - Flushing

Shoreline Village

Conveniently located along the water at Shoreline Village in Downtown Long Beach, Bicycles Queens - Flushing has been voted Long Beach’s favorite bike rental company! With over 25 years of experience, we feature the largest selection of bikes, specialty cycles, electric bikes, Surreys, cruiser bikes, and more – guaranteed fun for the whole family!

Hourly and long-term bike rentals available. We also offer a bike tour of Long Beach where you can explore the area at your own pace. Our location offers easy parking, direct access to the shoreline bike path, and lots of restaurants and shops in the area.

Rent a Surrey and ride with your whole family, or rent an electric bike to cover more ground without breaking a sweat. Come feel like a kid again as you ride along the boardwalk with the Pacific Ocean breeze in your hair. Bike locks, maps, and helmets are always included FREE with rentals. We also validate parking in the Shoreline Village lot.

Staying physically active is one of the best ways to keep your mind and body healthy. Low-contact outdoor activities like bike riding is a great way to get your body moving, and relieve stress while getting fresh air.
