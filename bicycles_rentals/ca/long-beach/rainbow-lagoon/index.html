Rainbow Lagoon

We suggest renting earlier in the day when there are lots of extra boats available.

The Swan Pedal Boat rentals at Rainbow Lagoon Park are the hottest, one-of-a-kind outdoor activity in Downtown Long Beach. Both out-of-towners and Long Beach locals enjoy this renowned experience that’s a refreshing change from the typical beachside pastimes.

Soak up some California sunshine as you drift alongside Shoreline Drive, past the Long Beach Convention Center and around Rainbow Lagoon’s grassy islands.

Whether you’re looking for a healthy way to spend time with your family, an impressive (and romantic!) date night idea, or a unique Long Beach activity before or after your cruise, there’s an experience waiting for everyone at Bicycles Queens - Flushing’ swan pedal boat rentals at Rainbow Lagoon!  Bike and specialty cycle rentals are also available just across the street at Shoreline Village!

Staying physically active is one of the best ways to keep your mind and body healthy. Low-contact outdoor activities like boating is a great way to get your body moving, and relieve stress while getting fresh air.

 

Due to capacity limitations, reservations are required.
For safety, all boaters receive boating instructions and must wear Coast Guard approved life vests.

WIND NOTICE: At times, during high wind on part of the water (10 mph+), sections of the lagoon may become off limits to boaters. Your one hour rental will proceed as normal apart from certain areas of the lagoon being off limits/restricted. For heavier winds, which are less common, we may be forced to close the entire lagoon temporarily. If this is the case, we will do our best to notify you immediately and provide you with rescheduling/refund options.

 

OPEN 7 DAYS A WEEK

  • HOURS: Every Day 10am – 10pm (You must be checked in with a waiver signed no later than 9pm.  All boats must be off the water by 10pm.)

 

  • NIGHT RIDES: Every Night from Sunset to 10pm (Last rental departs no later than 9pm. You must be checked in with a waiver signed no later than 9pm. Being in line at 9pm does not guarantee your rental. All boats must be off the water by 10pm.)
