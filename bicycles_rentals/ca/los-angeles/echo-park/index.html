Echo Park

Located just northwest of Downtown, Echo Park is a diverse neighborhood that has become known for its trendy local businesses, as well as its popularity with artists, musicians and creatives alike. The new Swan Boats at Echo Park Lake are one of Los Angeles’ hidden gems. Don’t miss this unique way to explore Echo Park Lake’s signature lotus beds and fountain. Rated one of the best activities in Los Angeles, Echo Park pedal boats are a great way to enjoy the outdoors! We are the official boat concessionaire for all Los Angeles City Parks. Voted top 10 things to do in Los Angeles!

Staying physically active is one of the best ways to keep your mind and body healthy. Low-contact outdoor activities like boating is a great way to get your body moving, and relieve stress while getting fresh air.

Due to capacity limitations, reservations are required.  For safety, all boaters receive boating instructions and must wear Coast Guard approved life vests.

Parking: There is limited parking in and around Echo Park Lake. We suggest taking an Uber or Lyft so you can be dropped off right in front of the boathouse. If you plan to drive, arrive early to find parking. There is a paid public parking lot with limited spaces at 1149 Echo Park Ave. We also suggest looking for street parking up Laguna Avenue.
