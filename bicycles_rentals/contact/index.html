Annual Pass

Get 50% off all rentals for a year with the purchase of an Annual Pass! The card pays for itself in one visit. For just a small fee, plus your full priced rental fee, you become an annual pass member which gives you 50% off all future rentals for one year.

Big Brothers Big Sisters Program

Bicycles Queens - Flushing supports Big Brothers Big Sisters by offering free rentals to the volunteers and the underprivileged children they mentor. This program, named “Biking Buddies,” was founded in 2006 and has continued to grow. For eleven years, we have offered free rental use to tens of thousands of Big Brothers Big Sisters matches across the nation.
