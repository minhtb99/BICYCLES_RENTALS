Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 8, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 8, 2026

Thank you for choosing Bicycles Queens - Flushing LLC., doing business as Bicycles Queens - Flushing ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please contact us at info@bicyclesqueens.com.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website wheelfunrentals.com and use our services. Please read it carefully. If you disagree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of our services immediately.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly: When you register, make a reservation, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Billing / payment information (processed securely through third-party processors)
  • Messages or inquiries you send us

Information collected automatically: When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain technical data, including:

  • IP address and general geolocation
  • Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Pages viewed, links clicked, and time spent on pages
  • Referring URLs and search terms

Payment data is processed via Peek.com (for online reservations) and Clover (for on-site payments). We do not store raw credit card numbers. Refer to their respective privacy policies: Peek Privacy | Clover Privacy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process reservations and payments
  • Send booking confirmations and service-related communications
  • Respond to inquiries and customer support requests
  • Send marketing emails (you can opt out at any time)
  • Display personalized content and advertising
  • Analyze website usage to improve our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Will Your Information Be Shared?

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share data in the following circumstances:

Basis Description
Service Providers Third-party vendors who process data on our behalf (payment, analytics, email delivery)
Legal Obligations When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
Vital Interests To prevent fraud, protect safety, or investigate policy violations
Business Transfers In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of company assets
With Your Consent Any other sharing with your explicit permission

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and serve relevant advertising. Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Marketing cookies — used to deliver relevant advertisements

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect some features of our website. See also our Cookie Notice.

5. How Long We Keep Your Information

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer period is required by law (e.g., tax or accounting requirements). When your data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — request your data in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Objection / Restriction — object to or restrict certain processing activities
  • Withdraw Consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@bicyclesqueens.com.

EEA / UK residents may also lodge a complaint with their local data protection authority. Swiss residents: FDPIC.

Opting out of marketing emails: Click the unsubscribe link in any marketing email, or email info@bicyclesqueens.com. You will still receive transactional/service emails.

7. Do-Not-Track Controls

Some browsers include a Do-Not-Track ("DNT") signal. Currently, no uniform standard exists for recognising DNT signals, and we do not respond to them. If a standard is adopted in the future, we will update this policy accordingly.

8. California Residents — CCPA

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know — categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you
  • Right to Delete — request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal exceptions)
  • Right to Opt-Out — we do not sell personal information, so this right does not currently apply
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

To submit a CCPA request, email info@bicyclesqueens.com. We will verify your identity before fulfilling the request.

Under California Civil Code §1798.83 ("Shine the Light"), you may also request information about categories of personal data we shared with third parties for direct marketing in the past year. Submit requests in writing to the address below.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated via a prominent notice on our website or by direct notification. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
