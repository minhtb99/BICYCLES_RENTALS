Welcome to

Bicycles Queens - Flushing

Heritage Square

Welcome to Bicycles Queens - Flushing at Seaside’s Heritage Square. Your adventure quota will be filled with our large selection of bike rental offerings, for one or up to 11 passengers. Explore Seasides Promenade, with more points of interest along the way! Enjoy your day on one of our icons Surreys, Duece Coupes, or traditional cruisers.
