Discover City Park by Bike

Welcome to

Bicycles Queens - Flushing

New Orleans City Park on Big Lake

Welcome to Bicycles Queens - Flushing at New Orleans City Park, your premier bike rental destination in The Big Easy! Explore the enchanting greenery, picturesque water features, and captivating sculptures of City Park aboard our flagship 4-wheel Surrey cycle. Discover the park at your own pace with our diverse selection of specialty cycles, cruiser bikes, kids bike and city bikes, perfect for a self-guided bike tour of New Orleans.

For a more relaxing pace, locals and visitors can glide across the lake in our iconic Swan Pedal Boats, including the magical Swan Boat Night Rides illuminated with LEDs. We also offer kayaks & canoes for exploring the canals and waterways of the park. All rentals are first come, first served. No reservations needed, we have plenty of bikes and boats, so come on by!

Featuring excellent customer service, over 25 years of experience and conveniently situated by the shores of Big Lake, Bicycles Queens - Flushing provides easy access to parking, restrooms, and everything you need for an unforgettable day at New Orleans City Park.
